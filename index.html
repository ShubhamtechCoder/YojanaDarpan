Compliance & Licensing Guide

Business Registration Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Choose a business structure
  2. Obtain Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
  3. Apply for Director Identification Number (DIN)
  4. Reserve your company name
  5. File incorporation documents
  6. Pay the required registration fees
  7. Obtain Certificate of Incorporation
  8. Apply for PAN and TAN
  9. Register for GST if applicable
  10. Open a business bank account
MCA Portal GST Portal

License Application Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Identify required licenses
  2. Check eligibility criteria
  3. Gather necessary documents
  4. Create account on government portal
  5. Fill out application form
  6. Upload required documents
  7. Pay application fees
  8. Submit application
  9. Track application status
  10. Download license certificate
FSSAI Portal Udyam Portal

Approval Filing Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Identify required approvals
  2. Check regulatory authority
  3. Prepare documentation
  4. Submit preliminary applications
  5. Complete main application
  6. Get required inspections
  7. Submit complete application
  8. Pay processing fees
  9. Respond to queries
  10. Receive approval certificate
